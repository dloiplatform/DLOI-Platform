The Solution

One platform. Every step.

DLOI handles the complete CRE transaction lifecycle — from first offer to closed title — entirely on-chain. Five steps. 4.2 days. Zero paper.

1
LOI Submission

Digital Letter of Intent — On-Chain

Buyer submits a DLOI on the XRP Ledger. AI drafts the Letter of Intent in 60 seconds using property data, market comps, and buyer terms. No attorneys needed for the first draft. Immutable record from day one.

2
RLUSD Escrow

Earnest Money Auto-Escrowed in RLUSD

The moment a DLOI is submitted, earnest money locks automatically in an XRPL smart contract in RLUSD — 1:1 USD, always stable. No wire transfers. No manual escrow agents. Visible on-chain to all parties instantly.

3
AI Contracts

AI-Drafted Purchase and Sale Agreement

Claude AI generates the PSA from DLOI terms. Certified DLOI attorney partners review and approve — earning $500–2,000 per review instead of drafting from scratch. Faster, cheaper, fully documented.

4
Title NFT

Title Deed Minted as NFT at Closing

Property deed minted as an NFT on XRPL using the XLS-20 standard. Ownership transfers atomically with RLUSD payment in a single transaction. 3.8-second finality. $0.000012 gas. Immutable ownership record — forever.

5
RWA Tokenization

Fractional Ownership via RWA Tokens

Properties tokenized as fractional ownership tokens under Reg D 506(b). Each token = $1,000 RLUSD. Monthly distributions paid directly to investor XRPL wallets in RLUSD. The institutional CRE market — now accessible.
